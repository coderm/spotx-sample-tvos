Inherits from NSObject
Declared in SPXAdController.h

Overview

SPXAdController manages inserting an ad and tracks its status during playback. For convenience, a number of placements are supported directly. For more complicated ad placements, the underlying AVPlayerItem is available.

NOTE: If you choose to place the AVPlayerItem in a player manually, you must pass that player to attachToPlayer:.
